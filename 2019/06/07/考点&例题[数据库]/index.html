一、求闭包

  • 例题

    1. 已知关系模式$R<U, F>$, 其中:

      $U = {A, B, C, D, E}$

      $F = {AB \to C, B \to C, C \to E, EC \to B, AC \to B }$

      求$(AB)^{+}_{F}$

      • 解
        1. $(AB)^{+}_{F} = {A, B}$
        2. ${AB \to C, B \to D} \Rightarrow {C, D}, \quad (AB)^{+}_{F} = {A, B, C, D}$
        3. ${C \to E} \Rightarrow {E}, \quad (AB)^{+}_{F} = {A, B, C, D, E}$

    2. 已知关系模式$R<U, F>$, 其中:

      $U = {A, B, C, D, E, I}$

      $F = {A \to D, AB \to E, BI \to E, CD \to I, E \to C}$

      求$(AE)^{+}_{F}$

      • 解: $(AE)^{+}_{F} = {A, E, C, D, I}$

    3. 已知$F = {A \to B, B \to C, A \to D, E \to F}, (A)^{+} = {A, B, C, D}$

二、拆解并筛选多余函数依赖

  • 例题
    1. 已知关系模式$R<U, F>$, 其中:

      $U={A, B, C, D, E, G}$

      $F={B \rightarrow D, D G \rightarrow C, B D \rightarrow E, A G \rightarrow B, A D G \rightarrow B C}$
      • 拆解: $ADG \to BC \Rightarrow \begin{cases}ADG \rightarrow B \ ADG \rightarrow C\end{cases}$
      • $F’ = {B \to D, G \to C, BD \to E, AG \to B, ADG \to B, ADG \to C}$
      • 筛选多余函数依赖:
        • 去除$B \to D$, $F’’ = {G \to C, BD \to E, AG \to B, ADG \to B, ADG \to C}$, $B^{+}_{F’’} = D$, 不包含推导式右侧元素, 保留.
        • 去除$AG \to B$, $F’’ = {B \to D, G \to C, BD \to E, ADG \to B, ADG \to C}$, $(ADG)^{+}_{F’’} = {A, D, G, C, B, D}$, 包含产生式右侧元素, 去除.
      • $F’’ = {B \to D, DG \to C, BD \to E, AG \to B}$
